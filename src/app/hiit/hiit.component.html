High-Intensity Interval Training

  • TV-PG

HIIT workouts are fast-paced, intense training sessions designed to maximize fat burning and improve both aerobic and anaerobic endurance. HIIT alternates between short bursts of high-intensity exercise (like sprinting, jumping, or burpees) followed by brief periods of rest or low-intensity exercise. This type of workout is effective for burning fat in a shorter amount of time, and it continues to burn calories even after the workout has ended due to the afterburn effect (EPOC - Excess Post-Exercise Oxygen Consumption)
